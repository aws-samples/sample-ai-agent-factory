Choose the setup path that matches how you are accessing this workshop:

- **[At an AWS Event](aws-event/)** — You are attending an AWS Immersion Day, workshop, or similar instructor-led event where a temporary AWS account is provided for you.
- **[Self-Paced Setup](self-service/)** — You are running this workshop independently in your own AWS account. A scripted deployer provisions the full platform (including the browser IDE) into your account in your chosen supported region (default `us-west-2`).
