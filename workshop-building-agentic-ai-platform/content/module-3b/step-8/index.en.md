::alert[Module 3b resources (Registry, Gateway, Cognito, guardrails) are used by Module 4. **Do not clean up now** — proceed to Module 4.]{type="warning"}

When you have completed the entire workshop, follow the instructions in the [Workshop Cleanup](../../cleanup/) section to tear down all resources. That section covers:

- Bedrock Guardrail deletion
- Policy Engine and Cedar policies (if created)
- OAuth2 Credential Provider (if created)
- Registry records and the registry itself
- CloudFormation stack (self-paced only)
